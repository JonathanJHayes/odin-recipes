Pasta Carbonara

Description

Pasta Carbonara is a classic and easy to make Italian dish, with crunchy pork and a creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 350g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 50g parmesan
  • 50g Pecorino
  • 2 Peeled garlic cloves
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil.
  2. Chop the pancetta into small cubes, removing the rind.
  3. Grate the parmesan and Pecorino into a bowl and mix.
  4. Put the Spaghetti into the boiling water, bring back to a boil and then simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. While the Spaghetti is boiling fry the pancetta in a large frying pan.
  6. Beat the eggs into the cheese mixture.
  7. Drain the Spaghetti and retain a small cup of pasta water
  8. Turn the heat of on the pancetta pan and add the Spaghetti, the egg cheese mixture and the cup of pasta water and stir.
  9. Serve.
